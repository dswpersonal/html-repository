The Best Movies According To David

My top 3 movies of all time!

Highlander

Highlander (1986) is a cult classic fantasy-action film focusing on Connor MacLeod (Christopher Lambert), an immortal Scottish warrior battling other immortals across centuries to win "The Prize," culminating in 1980s New York City. Known for its Queen soundtrack, dramatic visuals, and stylized sword fights, it is celebrated for its ambitious, romantic concept despite some dated effects and a messy, disjointed narrative.

Star Wars

Star Wars (1977) is a seminal space opera following farm boy Luke Skywalker as he joins the Rebel Alliance to destroy the Galactic Empire's superweapon, the Death Star. Guided by Obi-Wan Kenobi and aided by Han Solo, Luke masters the Force to fight Darth Vader. The Matrix

The Matrix (1999) is a groundbreaking sci-fi action film where hacker Neo (Keanu Reeves) discovers his reality is a simulation designed by machines to enslave humanity. Recruited by rebels led by Morpheus (Laurence Fishburne), Neo learns to bend the simulation's rules, embracing his role as "The One" to fight sentient Agents.
